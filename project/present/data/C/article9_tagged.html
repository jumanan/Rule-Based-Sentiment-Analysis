, the beleaguered financial giant, is scheduled to report first quarter 2010 results before the market opens on Monday, April 19.Citigroup Inc. is a global diversified financial services holding company.The Company provides consumers, corporations, governments and institutions with a range of financial products and services.As of December 31, 2009, Citigroup had approximately 200 million customer accounts and did business in more than 140 countries.Citigroup operates through two primary business segments: Citicorp, consisting of its Regional Consumer Banking RCB businesses and Institutional Clients Group ICG , and Citi Holdings, consisting of its Brokerage and Asset Management BAM , Local Consumer Lending LCL , and Special Asset Pool SAP .In April 2010, Barclays PLC acquired Italian credit card business of Citibank International Bank plc.In the fourth quarter of 2009, Citigroup disappointed investors by reporting earnings per loss share of $0.33 which exceeded analysts' consensus estimate $0.326 by $0.004.Citicorp fourth quarter revenues were $11.7 billion, down from $13.0 billion in the prior quarter.Managed revenues were $13.4 billion, down from $14.8 billion in the prior quarter.Excluding the impact of credit value adjustments CVA in each quarter, managed revenues were down approximately 7% sequentially, due primarily to declines in S&B revenues.The fourth quarter 2009 net loss was $7.6 billion, or $0.33 per share.Excluding the $6.2 billion after tax loss associated with TARP repayment and exiting the loss sharing agreement, the fourth quarter net loss was $1.4 billion or $0.06 per share.Fourth quarter net credit losses of $7.1 billion were down $0.8 billion from the prior quarter, marking the second consecutive quarter of improvement.Managed net credit losses were $10.0 billion, down from $11.0 billion in the prior quarter.For the 1st qtr 2010, analysts' estimates range from $0.08 to $0.04, with a consensus estimate of $0.001 .The consensus EPS forecast has decreased over the past week from $0.000 to $0.001.Downward revision in the analysts' consensus estimate is attributable to more than expected credit losses due both to consumers and businesses.Citi completed 14 divestitures in Citi Holdings in 2009, including Smith Barney, Nikko Cordial Securities and Nikko Asset Management.In January 2010, Citi Property Investors, a New York based real estate investment firm, announced that it was seeking buyera buyer for the company.In February 2010, Citigroup Inc announced its plans to spin off its Citi Private Equity's unit, a New York based provider of private equity fund services to its shareholders.On Monday, the stock closed trading at $4.26 compared to the 52 week range of $2.55 $5.43.In the last three months, the stock gained around $0.73 or 20.68%.The company's earnings expansion could support a price in excess of $4.7 by end 2010.Research Related Stocks: Rate this Commentary
