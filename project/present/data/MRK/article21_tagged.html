Santarus reports Schering Plough's launch of Zegerid products
Posted on: Fri, 02 Apr 2010 09:45:57 EDT
, Do you know when to trade & ?Check for a from TradingMarkets Apr 02, 2010 Datamonitor via COMTEX Santarus, a specialty biopharmaceutical company, has reported that Schering Plough HealthCare Products, the consumer healthcare subsidiary of Merck & Co, has commenced promotion of Zegerid OTC under its licensing agreement with Santarus for the over the counter, or OTC, market in North America.Under the terms of the Zegerid OTC license agreement, Santarus is entitled to a low double digit royalty, subject to adjustment in certain circumstances, on net sales of any Zegerid OTC products sold in North America.In turn, Santarus will be obligated to pay royalties to the University of Missouri on net sales of any such OTC products.Santarus also may be entitled to receive up to an additional $37.5 million in sales milestones.According to Santarus, Zegerid OTC is the only OTC proton pump inhibitor PPI product with two active ingredients, omeprazole, a PPI, and sodium bicarbonate.Gerald Proehl, president and CEO of Santarus, said: "The commercialization of Zegerid OTC is in keeping with our strategy to maximize our patented PPI technology.With its dual ingredient formula, Zegerid OTC offers an attractive alternative to the currently marketed OTC PPI products and positions us to receive royalty revenues without further investment from Santarus.Schering Plough Consumer HealthCare began shipping Zegerid OTC in late March, and we expect to report royalty revenue from these shipments in our first quarter financial results." http://www.datamonitor.com
