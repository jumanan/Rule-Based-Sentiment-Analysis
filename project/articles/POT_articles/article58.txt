 Potash Corporation of Saskatchewan Inc. Raises Q1 2010 EPS Guidance
Thursday, 11 Mar 2010 06:22pm EST
Potash Corporation of Saskatchewan Inc. revised first quarter of 2010 guidance and expects earnings to be in the range of $1.30-$1.50, well above the initial guidance of $0.70-$1.00 per share provided on January 28, 2010. The upward revision reflects a rebound in potash demand that is expected to drive a record quarter for North American sales volumes and strong offshore shipments, as well as higher-than-expected margins in nitrogen and phosphate. According to Reuters Estimates, analysts were expecting the Company to report EPS of $0.90 for the first quarter of 2010.

 Potash Corporation of Saskatchewan Inc. Prices Offering Of USD500 Million Six Year Notes And USD500 Million 10.5 Year Notes
Wednesday, 23 Sep 2009 05:27pm EDT

 Potash Corporation of Saskatchewan Inc. Lowers FY 2009 EPS Outlook; Sees Q3 2009 EPS At Low End Of Prior Outlook Range
Friday, 18 Sep 2009 05:45pm EDT

According to Reuters Estimates, analysts were expecting the Company to report EPS of $0.89 for third quarter of 2009 and EPS of $4.12 for fiscal 2009. 
 Potash Marketing Consortium Of Potash Corporation Of Saskatchewan Inc., The Mosaic Company and Agrium Inc. Agreed To Sell Potash To Indian Buyers-Reuters
Thursday, 23 Jul 2009 11:47am EDT
Reuters reported that the potash marketing consortium of Potash Corporation of Saskatchewan Inc., The Mosaic Company and Agrium Inc. agreed to sell potash to Indian buyers at $460 a tonne. Mosaic Co confirmed that Canpotex, the marketing consortium, also agreed to sell potash to Indian importers at the same price as its overseas competitors. The consortium concluded contracts in India totaling 850,000 tonnes with both its private and public sector partners.

 Potash Corporation of Saskatchewan Inc. Lowers Q2 2009 EPS Outlook
