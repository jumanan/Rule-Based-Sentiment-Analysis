A post earnings checkup on Agrium Inc.; AGU, TRA, CF, POT Wednesday, 10 March 2010 04:09 Earnings season can make investing tricky.Many investors try to time trades based on earnings announcements, but usually find such trading is inconsistent and risky.It is often better to take a look at how the market has reacted to a company’s results a few weeks after the initial announcement.Agrium Inc. AGU released its earnings filing on 02/09.The company reported a change in quarter over quarter sales of 40.76% and posted an EPS trailing twelve months of 2.92.One way to gauge performance is look at a stock compared to other stocks in its industry with similar market caps.AGU peer Terra Industries Inc. TRA has seen a 43.13% stock price gain over about the last month, while another peer, CF Industries Holdings, Inc. CF saw a 6.91% gain.So with a return of 14.97%, Agrium Inc. saw less price gain than TRA and beat CF’s price performance over the last month.Now, let’s see how Agrium Inc. stock performance compares to the rest of the market by looking at it compared to the Standard & Poor’s 500 Index .INX .Since 02/09, the S&P 500 index has returned around 6.5%, and again, AGU saw about a 14.97% gain during that time.Not bad.Compared to the rest of the “Agricultural Chemicals” industry
Since the AGU announcement about 30 days ago , the stock has posted a 14.97% gain.Over that same period, the stock’s industry, Agricultural Chemicals, saw a 6.40% gain.That means AGU that has outperformed its industry as a whole 133.91% since the earnings announcement.Small differences aren’t significant, but when the spread is large it indicates the stock is either much more or much less favored than its group as a whole.
