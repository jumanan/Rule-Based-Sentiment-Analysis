Short Term Speculators Expect Extended Winning Streak for Wynn Resorts, Limited
Andrea Kramer akramer@sir inc.com 3/23/2010 12:50:28 PM Wynn Resorts, Limited WYNN saw call volume jump to nearly twice the expected levels on Monday, as casino stocks gained ground in the wake of an upbeat analyst endorsement for Las Vegas Sands Corp. LVS .By the closing bell, the stock had seen close to 11,000 contracts change hands, compared to its predicted single session volume of fewer than 6,000 contracts.The equity's out of the money April 80 call garnered the most attention, with almost 3,000 contracts exchanged.Almost half of the front month calls traded closer to the ask price, implying they were likely bought, and call open interest at the round number strike advanced by roughly 1,300 contracts overnight.In other words, it appears near term traders were initiating bullish bets at the April 80 strike.Yesterday's influx in call volume echoes the recent trend on the International Securities Exchange ISE , where speculators during the past two weeks have bought to open more than twice as many WYNN calls as puts.In fact, the security's 10 day call/put volume ratio of 2.06 ranks in the 97th percentile of its annual range, suggesting that traders on the ISE have scooped up WYNN calls over puts at a pace only 3% of the time during the past year.In that same vein, the stock's Schaeffer's put/call open interest ratio SOIR has plunged to 1.18, in the 38th annual percentile.In other words, short term option players have been optimistically aligned toward the gaming guru only 38% of the time during the past 52 weeks.After tagging a new high of $77.41, WYNN has since given back 1.5% to linger in the $75.40 region.View Most Recent Blogs
