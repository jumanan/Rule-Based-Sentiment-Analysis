Alcoa Amends Its Deal With Saudi Arabian Mining

By The Associated Press 04/05/10 01:50 PM EDT
  • Loading Comments...
Stock quotes in this article: AA

NEW YORK AP — Aluminum producer Alcoa Inc. amended a deal with the Saudi Arabian Mining Co. related to the construction of a bauxite mine, alumina refinery, aluminum smelter and rolling mill in Saudi Arabia, according to a regulatory filing on Monday.
