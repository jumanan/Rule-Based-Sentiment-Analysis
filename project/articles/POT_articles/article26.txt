Canadian Stock Index Erases Loss as Potash Corp., RIM Advance
March 22, 2010, 12:58 PM EDT
More From Businessweek
 March 22 (Bloomberg) -- Canada&rsquo;s main stock index rose, erasing an earlier loss, as Potash Corp. of Saskatchewan Inc. and Research In Motion Ltd. gained. 
 The Standard & Poor&rsquo;s/TSX Composite Index rose 3.63 points, or less than 0.1 percent, to 11,951.61 at 12:37 p.m. in Toronto, after falling as much as 0.9 percent earlier.
 Potash Corp., the biggest fertilizer maker, rose 1.2 percent to C$124.54. RIM, maker of the BlackBerry smartphone, advanced 1.3 percent to C$75.
