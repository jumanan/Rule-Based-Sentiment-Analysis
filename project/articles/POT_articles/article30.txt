March 17, 2010 8:52 AM EDT
An analyst at Morgan Joseph this morning lifted his price target on shares of Potash (NYSE: 
) from $130 to $150 and also reiterated a Buy rating. Notably, Morgan Joseph upgraded shares of Potash to Buy as recently as March 8.
 The firm notes that last week's 
 from Potash was given as the company is "feeling more optimistic in the wake of strong demand for potash as the spring planting season gets closer." The Morgan Joseph analyst also argues that Potash is "back on track in terms of trend-line growth of roughly 2% per year worldwide." 
 Morgan Joseph raises its FY10 EPS estimate from $5.00 to $5.50 and its FY11 estimate from $7.00 to $8.00. The Street is currently looking for FY10 and FY11 EPS of $5.37 and $7.35, respectively.
 Shares of Potash are indicated almost 1% higher this morning. The stock last traded at $128.20. Based on today's price action, Morgan Joseph's new price target suggests potential upside of about 17%.
