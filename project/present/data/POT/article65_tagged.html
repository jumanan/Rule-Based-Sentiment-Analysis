Inside investor reaction to Syngenta AG recent earnings; SYT, MOS, POT, MON Tuesday, 09 March 2010 05:12 Earnings releases can make investing tricky.Many traders try to time trades based on earnings filings, but usually find such trading is difficult and dangerous.It is often better to take a look at how the market has reacted to a company’s results a few weeks after the initial announcement.Syngenta AG SYT released its earnings announcement on 02/05.The company reported a change in quarter over quarter sales of .18% and posted an EPS trailing twelve months of 2.92.One way to gauge performance is look at a stock compared to other stocks in its industry with similar market caps.SYT peer Mosaic Co. MOS has seen a 13.65% stock price gain over about the last month, while another peer, Potash Corp. of Saskatchewan, Inc. POT saw a 15.72% gain.So with a return of 11.37%, Syngenta AG saw less price gain than MOS and under performed POT’s price performance over the last month.Now, let’s see how Syngenta AG stock performance compares to the rest of the market by looking at it compared to the Standard & Poor’s 500 Index .INX .Since 02/05, the S&P 500 index has returned around 6.8%, and again, SYT saw about a 11.37% gain during that time.Not bad.Compared to the rest of the “Agricultural Chemicals” industry
Since the SYT announcement about 30 days ago , the stock has posted a 11.37% gain.Over that same period, the stock’s industry, Agricultural Chemicals, saw a 8.87% gain.That means SYT that has outperformed its industry as a whole 28.18% since the earnings announcement.Small differences aren’t significant, but when the spread is large it indicates the stock is either much more or much less favored than its group as a whole.
