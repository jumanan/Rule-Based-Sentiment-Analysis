Winners, losers in Potash NYSE: POT option strategies
you're watching: What's Mad About Options?An options related take on Jim Cramer's latest recommendations and analysis on the latest securities and ETFs getting attention on the Street.Options outcomes for bulls and bears on Potash
The Pick – Potash NYSE: host Jim Cramer said, “The fundamentals have gone bad, but the stock’s going higher.Something is going on, I don’t understand it.Maybe we’ll find out six months from now.” POT shares were trading at $127 at the time of this episode, up from $104.18 at the time of Cramer’s call.BULLISH Option Trade for POT:
Bullish option traders bought January 80 calls for $24.70 and sold January 105 calls for $6.70.Investors paid $18 for this spread, which expired with the stock at $115.14.This trade was a winner of $7, with the stock up $23.BEARISH Option Trade for POT:
Bearish investors bought March 95 puts for $6.20 and sold March 110 140 call spreads for $6.20 in a costless trade.This spread was trading for $17 just before March options expiration, making this trade a loser of $17 with the stock up $23.Keep in mind that investors who traded these option plays were subject to that affected overall gains and losses., you can build a profit/loss diagram to help visualize these trades.For more trading ideas and breaking news on the options market, stay tuned in to The Options News Network.Related Symbols Recent Coverage
