NYSE: C has already implemented a similar policy.While this move will save consumers from surprise hits to their accounts, this will cost BofA millions in fee income as more than 60% of all overdraft fees charged by the bank are for debit card transactions.The new overdraft rule will apply to new customers from June 19, 2010 and for existing customers effective August.However, BofA customers still could withdraw more than their balance from ATMs, but they will have to pay a $35 overdraft fee.Last week, the Treasury received net proceeds of $1.5 billion from the sale of warrants entitling it to
